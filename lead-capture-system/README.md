# Smart Lead Capture System

An automated lead-capture workflow that ensures no inquiry goes unanswered.

## What it does
- Captures form submissions via webhook
- Instantly sends the lead a professional welcome email
- Logs every lead to Google Sheets with timestamp and status
- Alerts the business owner in real time via Telegram and WhatsApp (Twilio)
- Sends the owner a detailed email notification for fast follow-up

## Why it matters
Most businesses lose leads in the first hour — a form fills at night, nobody replies until morning, the customer moves on. This system closes that gap automatically, so every lead gets an instant response and the owner never misses one.

## Built with
n8n · Google Sheets · Gmail · Telegram · Twilio (WhatsApp) · Webhooks
